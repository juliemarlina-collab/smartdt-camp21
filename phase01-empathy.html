T02 Interview Guide

Step 2 of Empathy: prepare your questions before you conduct and observe.

💬
Good interviews move from easy to deep, then dig further.
Warm-Up → Deep-Dive → Probes / Follow-Up
✍️
What to writeGoal, questions at each level, roles and materials.
⚠️
Common mistakeAsking leading or closed (yes/no) questions.
✅
Enough whenAt least 2 questions per tier, 6 total.
👤
Mini case"What part of cleaning this hall takes the most time?"
💬
Warm-Up Questions

Used to break the ice and make the user comfortable.

  • Who are you?
  • What do you usually do here?
  • Have you experienced this problem before?
❓
Deep-Dive Questions

Used to understand reasons, feelings and challenges.

  • Why is this difficult for you?
  • How do you usually handle it?
  • What frustrates you the most?
🔍
Probes / Follow-Up

Used to go deeper.

  • Can you explain more?
  • What happened next?
  • How did that make you feel?
💛 Reminder: good empathy questions are open-ended, respectful, and focused on the user's real experience.
