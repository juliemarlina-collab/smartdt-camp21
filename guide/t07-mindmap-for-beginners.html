Mind Map & Ideation

Ideate  |  Phase 03  |  CAMP21 Smart DT Project
1 What is ideation?

Ideation is where you generate many ideas — think big, no limits, build on others. A mind map helps by connecting one central challenge to themes and sub-ideas so the team can branch out fast.

2 The 4 ideation rules
1 Go for quantity — many ideas first.
2 Defer judgement — don't filter yet.
3 Welcome wild ideas.
4 Build on each other's ideas.
3 The mind map — central challenge + 6 branches
Central challengeHow might we attract more young visitors to our craft area?
a) Customer / Visitor

Who are they? What do they want?

b) Product / Craft

Ideas for the crafts themselves.

c) Promotion / Awareness

How people hear about you.

d) Shopping Experience

How visiting & buying feels.

e) Payment / Access

How they pay and reach you.

f) New Idea / Opportunity

Fresh, unexpected directions.

7 Case study — Makcik, Hall Cleaner
🧕🏽
ChallengeHow might we help hall cleaners work safely and efficiently within a tight turnaround time?
Time branchFaster-drying methods, buffer time between events, staggered exits.
Tools branchBetter mop design, quick-dry mats, wet-floor sensors.
Safety branchColour-coded zones, clearer signs, floor markers.
💡 Most promising: a colour-coded wet-zone marker system showing estimated dry time.
