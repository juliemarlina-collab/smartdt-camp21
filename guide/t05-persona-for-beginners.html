1 What is a persona?

A persona is a believable profile of one representative user, built from your empathy evidence — not invented. It turns scattered findings (Say · Do · Think · Feel, quotes, pains) into a single human your team can design for.

2 Real vs Invented
✅ Evidence-based
"Hesitates before trying" — seen in T01.
"Scared to spoil the cloth" — quote from T03.
Needs reassurance — repeated pattern.
❌ Invented
"Loves bright colours" — never observed.
"Age 25, owns an iPhone" — guessed.
"Wants our app" — that's a solution.

Rule: every field comes from evidence, not imagination.

7 Case study — Makcik, Hall Cleaner persona
🧕🏽
Snapshot"Makcik", hall cleaner, mops Dewan Wawasan after every programme.
GoalFinish cleaning safely before the next group arrives.
PainsLarge hall takes long to mop; risk of slippery floor; tight turnaround between events.
NeedsThe right tools, a realistic time gap, and clear signage to warn people of wet floors.
💡 Opportunity: Help hall cleaners work faster and more safely within a tight turnaround time.
