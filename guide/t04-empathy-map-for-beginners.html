Empathy Map for Beginners

Empathise | Day 1 | CAMP21 Smart DT Project
1What is an empathy map?

An empathy map helps your team organise interview findings to understand what users say, do, think and feel.

2The 4 Main Areas

💬 Say

Exact quotes or important words from the user.

🏃 Do

Actions, behaviours or body language.

💭 Think

Possible beliefs, worries or hidden concerns.

❤️ Feel

Emotions such as fear, confusion or hope.

3How to Use It
1

Listen

Review interviews and notes.

2

Sort

Put evidence into the four areas.

3

Find Patterns

Look for pains and needs.

4

Use It

Prepare for Define phase.

4Case Study

Say

“This hall is big. It takes time to clean. I must finish before the next group arrives.”

Do

Sweeps rubbish, moves chairs, mops the main areas and places the wet floor sign.

Think

“Will the floor be wet and slippery? I need the right tools to make my work easier.”

Feel

Tired, worried someone may slip, proud when the hall is clean.

5Bridge to Define

User need: Makcik needs the right tools and a realistic time gap between programmes to clean the hall safely and confidently.

“Map the person, not just the problem.”
