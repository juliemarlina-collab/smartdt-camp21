T03

Transcription for Beginners

Empathise | Day 1 | CAMP21 Smart DT Project
1What is transcription?

Transcription turns your recorded interview into written words you can read, highlight and share as evidence.

2Clean vs Raw

🎙️ Raw

“Erm, I really wanted to try but, like, I was scared I’d spoil the cloth lah.”

✨ Clean

“I wanted to try, but I was scared I’d spoil the cloth.”

3Transcript Parts

🎧 Audio Info

File name, length, interviewee and place.

🏷️ Speaker Labels

Interviewer / Visitor / Artisan.

📄 Clean Transcript

The conversation written clearly.

❝ Key Quotes

Strong exact words saved as evidence.

4Steps
1

Listen

Play the full recording.

2

Write

Type what is said.

3

Clean

Remove noise, keep meaning.

4

Mark

Highlight key quotes.

5Case Study

Raw

“Erm, this hall very big one… take a long time to mop everything lah.”

Clean

“This hall is big. It takes a long time to mop everything.”

Key Quote

“It takes a long time to mop everything.”

Meaning

The size of the hall directly affects how much time Makcik needs to finish safely.

“Keep their words. Lose the noise.”
