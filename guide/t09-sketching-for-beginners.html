Idea Sketching

Ideate  |  Phase 03  |  CAMP21 Smart DT Project
1 What is idea sketching?

Sketching makes your chosen idea visible through quick drawings and notes — so the team and users can understand it fast. You don't need to be an artist: clarity beats beauty.

2 Clarity vs Pretty
✅ Aim for this
Clear boxes, arrows and labels.
Show how a user interacts.
Note the key features.
❌ Don't worry about
Perfect, beautiful drawing.
Shading and realism.
Getting it right first try.

Stick figures and boxes are totally fine — just label everything.
